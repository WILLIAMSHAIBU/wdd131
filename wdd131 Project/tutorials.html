How to Send Money

📞 1. Dial *211# (Airtel) or *444# (TNM)
💸 2. Choose "Send Money"
📱 3. Enter recipient's number
💰 4. Enter amount
🔐 5. Confirm with your PIN

How to Check Balance

📞 Dial *211# or *444#
📊 Choose "Balance Inquiry" to view your mobile wallet balance.

How to Buy Airtime

📞 1. Dial *211# or *444#
🛒 2. Choose "Buy Airtime"
👤 3. Select "Self" or "Others"
💰 4. Enter amount
🔐 5. Enter your PIN to confirm

How to Pay a Bill

📞 1. Dial *211# or *444#
🧾 2. Choose "Pay Bills"
🏛️ 3. Select the company (e.g., ESCOM, water board)
🔢 4. Enter customer/account number
💰 5. Enter amount
🔐 6. Confirm with your PIN

How to Withdraw Cash

📞 1. Dial *211# or *444#
🏧 2. Choose "Withdraw Money"
📍 3. Enter agent number
💰 4. Enter amount
🔐 5. Enter your PIN
✅ 6. Show confirmation code to agent
